Welcome to the Waste Free App Style Guide



The aim of this app was to create a sustainable tool that monitors and reminds the user of when their food is becoming and eventually is, inedible. The idea came from visiting my brothers house, in which I realised that he often forgot the food in his fridge that would expire and go to waste; both in consumption and money.



Here you will find the style guides of the application, for anyone that wants to know how it was made/styled. Enjoy!
